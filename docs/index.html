A fast, reliable, free, and open-source convex optimization solver.

SCS (Splitting Conic Solver) is a numerical optimization package for solving large-scale convex cone problems, based on our paper.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{lcr} \begin{array}{ll} \mbox{minimize} & \frac{1}{2} x^T P x + c^T x \\ \mbox{subject to} & A x + s = b\\ & s \in \mathcal{K} \end{array} &\quad& \begin{array}{ll} \mbox{minimize} & \frac{1}{2} x^T P x + c^T x \\ \mbox{subject to} & A x + s = b\\ & s \in \mathcal{K} \end{array} \end{array}\end{split}\]

with variables \(x \in \mathbf{R}^n\) and.

P positive semidefinit upper trianfular
